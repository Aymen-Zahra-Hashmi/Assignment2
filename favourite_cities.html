Islamabad

Islamabad is the capital city of Pakistan. It is the country's ninth-most populous city. It has always been my favorite place to spend my vacations as it has beautiful and calm spots to live there including Daman-e-Koh Park,Lake View Park,Faisal Mosque and many more.

Azad Kashmir

Azad Jammu and Kashmir abbreviated as AJK is the the most green and beautiful city of Pakistan. It has always been my favorite place to spend my vacations as it has beautiful and calm spots to live there including Neelum valley,Mangla Dam,Ganga choti(very cold weather there),Jari kas park and many more.

Lahore

Lahore is the widest and populated city of Pakistan and is popular for it's beautiful culture and views as well as tasty and delicious food. It has always been my favorite place to spend my vacations as it has beautiful and calm spots to live there including Badshahi Mosque,Minar-e-Pakistan,Shalimar Gardens,Lahore Museum,Lahore Fort and many more.
